AI Powered Virtual Assistant

This paper delves into the evolution and functionalities of AI-Powered virtual Assistants, presenting their profound impact on various sectors ranging from healthcare to customer service. The core of AI-Powered virtual Assistants lies in their ability to comprehend Natural Language and context, enabling Seamless Communication and task execution. Leveraging advanced Machine Learning Algorithms, these assistants continuously learn and adapt to user preferences, refining their Responses and Recommendations over time. Moreover, integration with Big Data analytics equips them with vast knowledge repositories, facilitating informed decision-making and personalized assistance. In addition to enhancing productivity and efficiency, AI-powered virtual assistants are revolutionizing accessibility and inclusivity, bridging the gap for individuals with disabilities and Language barriers.

Deepfake Detection System

Deepfake technology presents a formidable challenge to the authenticity of digital media, posing risks of misinformation and deception. In response, our research delves into the development of a machine learning-based deepfake detection system. Leveraging advanced algorithms and neural network architectures, our system aims to accurately identify manipulated media, contributing to the ongoing efforts to combat the spread of synthetic content online. This uses Machine learning and Deep Learning techniques with the help of pre trained models to identify deepfakes in videos and pictures that are widespreading and faking the information.
